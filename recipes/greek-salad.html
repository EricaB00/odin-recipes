Greek Salad

Description

A recipe to make a healthy and tasty Greek salad. It takes about 15 minutes to prepare and feeds about 6 people.

Ingredients

  • 3 large ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cucumbers, peeled and chopped
  • 1 small red onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 4 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 6 black Greek olives, pitted and sliced

Steps

  1. In a salad bowl, combine tomatoes, cucumber, and onions.
  2. Sprinkle the oil, lemon juice, oregano, and salt and pepper.
  3. Sprinkle the feta cheese and olives over the salad.
