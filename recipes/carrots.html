Honey Ginger Carrots

Description

This is an easy recipe to create tasty sweetned carrots with honey, lemon, and a hint of ginger. Total time to make is about 20 minutes and serves about 6 people.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of sliced carrots
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons of honey
  • pinch ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice, to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil and add carrots.
  2. Cook carrots until tender but firm, about 5 minutes, and then drain.
  3. In a large skillet over low heat, melt butter with honey and stir in ground ginger and lemon juice.
  4. In skillet stir in the carrots and simmer until heated through.
